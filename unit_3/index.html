Your Yoga School in Moratalaz

At Mangalam Yoga School we believe that traditional yoga, practiced and taught in India for thousands of years, is a complete system in itself and needs no additions or correction. It is a science that has been developed and perfected for centuries by many of the greatest and wise Masters of mankind. As soon as we begin our practice, we will begin to notice its benefits IMMEDIATELY, both physically and mentally.

    A yoga practice basically consists of:
  • Respiratory exercises
  • Adequate physical exercise
  • Relaxation
  • Concentration and meditation
